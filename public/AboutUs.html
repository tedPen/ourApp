About The Experiment


The social dinning experiment was built out of frustration.

Middlebury is full of interesting and diverse students. Midd students enjoy meeting new people and socializing with others. Midd students are articulate and friendly.


But for some reason.... After a certain point.... Midd kids stop making new friends.

We want to mix things up for a night. You might reconnect with an old friend, meet a new friend or just leave with a good story.
